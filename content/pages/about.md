bio_intro: >-
  Born into a world of colour and form, Sadaf has spent over a decade exploring
  the delicate tension between light, shadow, and emotional memory through
  paint.
bio_body: >-
  Her practice is rooted in an intuitive dialogue with the canvas — each stroke
  an inquiry into the space between perception and reality. Working primarily in
  oils, she builds luminous surfaces through layering, revealing depth that
  rewards close attention.


  Her work has been shown in solo and group exhibitions across multiple cities,
  and her paintings are held in private collections internationally.
artist_statement: >-
  I paint because I believe colour holds memory. Each canvas is an attempt to
  make that memory visible — to give form to something that exists between
  thought and feeling. 
studio_note: Currently working from her studio, where silence is the primary material.
